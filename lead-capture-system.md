# Lead Capture System (Google Forms + Zapier + Discord)

## Problem
Many small businesses lose potential clients because they don't respond fast enough to new leads.

## Solution
I built an automated lead capture system that instantly notifies the team when a new lead is submitted.

## Stack
- Google Forms (lead capture)
- Zapier (automation)
- Discord (real-time alerts)
- Google Sheets (basic CRM)

## How it works
1. A user submits a form
2. Data is stored in Google Sheets
3. A real-time notification is sent to Discord
4. The team can immediately follow up

## Result
- Faster response time
- Better lead management
- No missed opportunities

## Use cases
- Restaurants taking orders
- Consultants capturing leads
- Small businesses managing inquiries
